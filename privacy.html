Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

Welcome to Partner Life. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data, focusing on our commitment to automation and local processing.

1. Information We Collect

Partner Life is designed to minimize manual entry. We may process the following types of data:

  • Financial Data: Transaction details captured via notification scraping or manual input.
  • Personal Goals: Short-term and long-term milestones shared between partners.
  • Device Information: Basic technical data required for app functionality and synchronization.

2. How Data is Processed

We prioritize local-first processing. Where possible, data is analyzed directly on your device using offline models to ensure your sensitive financial and personal information remains under your control.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared between linked partners within the app to facilitate shared goal tracking and expense management.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transit and at rest. However, remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time directly through the app settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@partnerlife.app
